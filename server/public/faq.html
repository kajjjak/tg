Frequently asked questions

Passengers

How does client know when a driver has arrived
The app will send a notification to the customer when the driver has arrived
How does client know that a driver will arrive after 30 minutes
A notification is sent to the client when a driver starts the job. If there is a substantial delay. The driver can ask the operator to notify about the time delay (for example 30 minutes). A notification will be sent to the client and displayed in the app.
What languages are supported
The customers can select any language you want to support. You will be able to define all the text fields in the app.
How does a customer cancel a taxi
The customer will be displayed an button to call the operator if they want to make changes to the order. Then the operator can then relay the message to the driver.

Drivers

Does this mean that every taxi driver must have a mobile phone
No. Drivers that does not use mobile phones receave instructions from your operator as usual
How does a driver receave a new job
Through operator and/or app notification. If the driver is using the app, an notification will be sent to the driver.
How does a driver know if job is canceled
The drivers using the app will receave a notification that there is a change in his schedule and the job will be disabled

Operator

How can a operator see if a customer wants a taxi
The operator uses administrator interface and can see all the request being created real time on a map.
How can a operator delegate a job
The operator simply selects the job that is displayed on the map and chooses the driver. Then he/she may choose to contact the driver

Service

How fast will you be able to create the app
When all the information is submited it will take 1-2 weeks to finalize and deploy your app on Google Play (Android) and App Store (Apple devices)
Can I use it on iPad and tablets
Yes you can
What happens if I miss a payment
If there is an issue we will contact you and work out a solution
Can I try the app
You can try it for free one month. It is as simple as creating an account and filling in your app information
How fast can you remove the app
Approximatly 24 hours
What about Blackberry and Window phones
Currently we are focused on Android devices (34% market share) and Apple devices (55% market share)
Why so cheap
We aim to provide all taxi stations the ability to compete with Uber and Lyft
